Arena is the first of a CRPG game series called The Elder Scrolls, made by Bethesda Softworks. The project was led by Vijay Lakshman. The game boasts a 3D world you can walk and interact in, similar to Ultima: Underworld, as well as a variety of classes you can roleplay as, allowing many playstyles distinct from one another.
